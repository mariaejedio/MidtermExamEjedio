Festivals | Davao City

Araw ng Dabaw

"Araw ng Dabaw is a month-long celebration with various activities held in Davao every March. It's also the name of Davao City's Charter Day celebrated on March 16 annually."

Kadayawan Festival

"The Kadayawan Festival is an annual festival in the city of Davao in the Philippines. The festival is a celebration of life, a thanksgiving for the gifts of nature, the wealth of culture, the bounties of harvest and serenity of living."

The Pasko Fiesta sa Davao

"The Pasko Fiesta sa Davao is an annual event of the city government of Davao, in partnership with the private sector, to celebrate the Filipino tradition during the Holiday Season come the last quarter of the year," Duterte-Carpio said in the order.

Chinese New Year Festival in Davao City

The Chinese New Year Festival in Davao City. The only Chinatown in Mindanao is located in Davao City, so the town hosts the biggest celebration for the Chinese-Filipino or Chinoy communities in the Philippines.

Chinese Mid-Autumn Moon Festival

"Another major festival for the Filipino-Chinese community in Davao is the Chinese Mid-Autumn Moon Festival or Moon Cake Festival, for short. The tradition that happens every 1st of September or October encourages everyone to share a moon cake with their loved ones during a full moon."
